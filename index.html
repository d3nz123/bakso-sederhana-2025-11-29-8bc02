About Bakso Sederhana

From a Small Solo Kitchen to a Modern Bakso Experience

Bakso Sederhana began in 2005 as a humble family stall in Solo, where our founder’s parents served steaming bowls of bakso to neighbors, office workers, and students. Their simple philosophy—use fresh ingredients, cook with heart, and treat every guest like family—quickly turned a modest stall into a beloved local institution. As demand grew, we refined our processes while preserving the original recipes and techniques that kept customers coming back day after day.

Today, Bakso Sederhana combines that rich heritage with a modern, efficient dining experience. Our mission is to bring authentic Bakso Solo to more people without compromising on taste, quality, or warmth. We believe in honesty in our ingredients, consistency in our craft, and kindness in every interaction—whether you are dining in, grabbing a quick bowl between meetings, or ordering online for the family at home.

Our Principles in Every Bowl

  • Honest Ingredients

    We work closely with local suppliers to source fresh meat and produce every day—no hidden additives, no shortcuts.

  • Continuous Improvement

    We listen to customer feedback, refine our processes, and regularly update our menu to keep things exciting while staying true to our roots.

  • Consistent Quality

    Our kitchens follow clear standards for each component—broth, meatballs, noodles, toppings—so your experience remains reliably excellent.

  • Warm Hospitality

    From a friendly greeting at the counter to remembered favorite orders, we aim to make every visit feel like coming home.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our bakso, ingredients, or ordering process? Here are answers to the most common things people ask before their first (or fifth) bowl of Bakso Sederhana.

Authenticity for us starts with family recipes and ends with consistent execution. Our broth is based on a Solo-style stock that balances beef richness with aromatic spices, simmered for hours every morning—not made from instant powders. The meatballs are hand-rolled to achieve the classic Solo bakso texture: springy yet tender, never overly dense. We also use traditional accompaniments like mie, bihun, tahu, and siomay in proportions that stay true to how bakso is enjoyed in Solo, while serving it in a clean, modern setting.

Yes. Our production schedule is built around fresh, daily preparation. The broth is started early each morning, simmered low and slow for more than six hours, and used only for that day’s service. Meatballs are mixed and rolled in batches every day for optimal texture and flavor. We do not carry over broth or meatballs to the next day, and we avoid heavy MSG usage, so you can enjoy a comforting bowl that feels clean and satisfying, not overly salty or artificial.

Our kitchen workflow is designed to handle rush hours efficiently. For delivery orders, we work with trusted partners to keep end-to-end delivery within 30–40 minutes for most locations.
